About Us

Westlands Banquet Centre is the most sought-after banqueting centre in Nairobi. Ideally located on Westlands Road, right next to Graffins college. This is the most ideal place for your events.

With close proximity to Nairobi’s CBD, we are the ideal and most convenient banqueting venue for all conferences and events. Our spacious halls are large and spacious to facilitate all your event needs.

BANQUET HALL – PILLARLESS SPACES

We guarantee ample space that provides an amazing experience for you and your guests with a seating capacity of up to 750. To ensure that we provide the optimal event solutions, we have creatively and ingeniously designed our halls with zero barricading pillars.

This makes it the best venue to hold large formal events and social gatherings such as; symposiums, conferences, seminars, trade shows, birthdays, weddings, bridal showers, graduations among others.


OUTDOOR & INDOOR EXPERIENCES

Our indoor halls and rooms are exquisite with elegant designs featuring all over the rooms. They are also easily flexible to customization and come with; an air conditioner, free high speed internet, and ample space to accommodate large groups.

You can host :
  • Weddings
  • Parties
  • Baby Showers
  • Bridal Showers
  • Graduation Parties
  • Any Other Function

PARKING AREA

Our ample parking space is easily accessible and manageable to track and easily remember where you last parked your car.

This modern-day created parking has been design to accommodate a large number of vehicles regardless of size. We do not charge parking fees for holding events in the area.


COVID-19 COMPLIANCE

We prioritize your health and safety thus we have adhered to all Ministry of Health (MOH) regulations on the Covid-19 prevention guidelines. We ensure that social distancing is observed in all seating arrangements with stickers all over the property reminding you to keep distance and to wear a mask.

Hand sanitizers are also available all around the area to maintain the highest level of hygiene guaranteeing your health and safety.
